About the Game

Guess the Number is a fun and simple number guessing game. The rules are easy:

  • You start at Level 1 with 5 attempts.
  • Try to guess the hidden number between 1 and 100.
  • If you guess correctly, you level up!
  • If you fail, the game resets to Level 1.

All progress is saved while your browser tab is open, but resets when you close it.
